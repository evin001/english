### Lesson 51

> **guarantee** - гарантировать   
> **lead to** - приводить

_I won't do it_   
_I won't be against it_   
_You won't pass this exam without preparation_   
_They won't find a solution to this problem_

_She won't translate all the text without a dictionary_   
_It won't lead to success_

> **recommend** - рекомендовать

_I won't recommend this book_   
_I won't take part in this competition / tournament / championship / contest_

### Lesson 53

**Future Simple (Question)**

`Will + subject + verb?`

> **automatically** - автоматически   
> **attitude to/towards** - отношение к чему-либо   
> **point at** - указывать на что-либо   
> **last forever** - длиться вечно   
> **last for** - длиться какое-то время   
> **last for more** - длиться больше
> **last for about** - длиться около

_Will I have this level?_   
_Will we speak English correctly and automatically?_   
_Will it change their attitude to/towards this problem?_   
_Will you point at my mistakes?_   
_Will it last forever?_   
_Will it last for two hours?_   
_Will it last for more than two hours?_   
_Will it last for about two hours?_   
_Will it last for about a week?_   

> **the day after tomorrow** - после завтра   
> **in the end/beginning** - в конце/начале   
> **at the end/beginning** - в конце/начале чего-то   
> **available** - доступный   
> **tomorrow morning/evening** - завтрашнее утро/вечер   

_Will you see him the day after tomorrow?_   
_Will it be possible in the end?_   
_Will it be possible at the end of the meeting?_   
_Will you be available tomorrow morning?_  

> **financial problems** -  финансовые проблемы   
> **get dark** - темнеть   
> **sign** - подписать   
> **report** - отчёт   
> **commercial** - коммерческий   
> **threat** - угроза

_Will they have financial problems?_   
_Will it get dark soon?_   
_Will it be necessary to check it again?_   
_Will it be necessary to sign this report?_   
_Will they have a commercial success?_   
_Will it be a potential threat?_
